What We Thought

Mission

We aim to contribute to the fields of education and travel.

Our mission is to foster personal growth and meaningful experiences through education and travel in Japan.
Growing through education is a vital process. Both school-based learning and societal learning are equally important.
By international standards, Japan is one of the few countries with a literacy rate close to 100%, thanks to its robust education system and high emphasis on learning.
At the same time, Japan is a disaster-prone nation, and its people, having experienced many challenges, embody a spirit of mutual help and a "mottainai" mindset that values resources and the environment.
We believe this social environment offers valuable learning opportunities for international students.
The number of travelers to Japan from abroad is increasing. We believe travel is closely linked to learning and exploration, and we are dedicated to providing top-quality education services and accommodation experiences that allow guests to immerse themselves in Japanese life.

What We Do

Free Education Consulting

Education is a common concern for many families. In particular, some Chinese families in Japan lack sufficient understanding of the Japanese education system, making it harder to support their children's education.
We offer completely free education consulting services.

  • Consulting on children's Japanese language education
  • Consulting on children's Chinese language education
  • Consulting on tutoring services
  • Consulting on academic advancement

Study Abroad Support in Japan

Studying in Japan is a cost-effective choice.
