Why Vacation Rental Services

Vacation rentals are a relatively new service. In recent years, a trend has emerged from Western countries where homeowners temporarily rent out part of their homes to travelers. The reason for the popularity of such vacation rental services is believed to be a win-win business model for both owners and travelers.

While accommodation needs are originally diverse, hotels typically provide services to meet the majority of needs. Vacation rentals are not necessarily in competition with hotels. In rural areas where hotels don't exist, vacation rentals are often the only accommodation service available. Even in big cities, there's a demand from travelers who want to experience local residences, which is why vacation rental services exist.
