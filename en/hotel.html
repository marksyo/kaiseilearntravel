Why Homestay Services?

Accommodation needs vary widely, but hotels often prioritize efficiency and provide standardized services.
Homestays are not necessarily in competition with hotels.
There is a growing demand for families and groups to travel freely, experience local life, and do laundry as they would at home. This is why homestay services exist.

3. Homestay Management Services

Managing a homestay is not easy—it requires significant time, effort, and language skills to handle international guests.
We offer the following management services:

  • (1) Assistance with obtaining homestay permits
  • (2) Web registration and guest communication
  • (3) Cleaning and pre-check-in preparations
  • (4) Provision of equipment and linens

4. Management of Traditional Houses or Vacation Homes

Properly managing unused traditional houses or vacation homes can generate income for owners.
Having people stay in these properties brings back their original vibrancy, creating a positive impact.

  • (1) Revitalization and operation of vacant homes
  • (2) Management of traditional houses
  • (3) Management of vacation homes
